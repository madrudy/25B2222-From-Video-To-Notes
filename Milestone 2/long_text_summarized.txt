Yoshi's New Island is a 2014 platform game developed by Arzest and published by Nintendo for the Nintendo 3DS. It is the third installment in the Yoshi's Island series. The player characters are different-colored dinosaurs known as Yoshis. Yoshi's abilities include hovering in midair briefly, swallowing and spitting out enemies, and throwing eggs to attack foes and solve puzzles. The game was released in Europe, North America, and Australia in March 2014 and in Japan in July 2014. By 2020 it had sold more than two million copies worldwide.
